Currency Exchange Web App

Main Features:
    -ability to see the exchange rate between any two currency using an api
    -ability to compare to your local exchange rate
    -ability to see how much money you lose depending the sum you want to exchange localy
    -list of all suported currencies
    -all rates for a specified currency
    -historical rates for a specified currency

Other Features:
    -the ability to favorite particular exchange rates to your profile
    -the ability to create a account to save your favorites
    -a history to see how much money you lost during a specific period
